Appa

Developed and designed by Noah Cote

This is Appa, he is a flying bison with a range of emotions, which include:

  1. Relaxed
  2. Happy
  3. Sad
  4. Crying
  5. Hungry
  6. Tired
  7. Sleeping

Given Appa's various states, they are mostly effected by outside stimuli, below you will find actions you can take
in the forms of buttons. These buttons will help Appa transition between his different states, but there are also uncontrollable
factors that can affect him as well, such as time, lack of attention, and hunger. Above Appa a header displays the state in which
he is in, and below that is a hint on how to bring him back to his relaxed state. For more detailed documentation please visit Appa, A Virtual Pest's Documentation.
